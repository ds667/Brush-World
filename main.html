About Toothbrushes

Toothbrushes are essential tools for oral hygiene, used to clean teeth, gums, and tongue to prevent tooth decay and gum disease.

Types of Toothbrushes

  • Manual Toothbrush: Traditional and affordable, ideal for regular cleaning.
  • Electric Toothbrush: Powered brushes with vibration or rotation for deeper cleaning.
  • Soft-Bristled Toothbrush: Gentle on teeth and gums, recommended by dentists.
  • Interdental Toothbrush: For cleaning between teeth and around braces.
